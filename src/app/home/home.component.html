Introduction

This website is created for the c3.ai competition. On this website you can find the following:


CT-scan

  • Upload a chest CT-scan image to be investigated by our AI algorithm for COVID-19 detection.

Analytics

  • Correlation: Look at correlation between covid-19 and temperature, humidity and life expectancy. New York, Illinois and California are taken as examples.
  • Intervention: Look whether lockdown or face mask intervention has (positive) effect on the spread of covid-19. Again, New York, Illinois and California are taken as examples.
  • Summary: This page shows the infection and death percentiles per category relation (gender, age and other diseases). It includes over half million anonymised patient data released by the Mexican government.
  • Forecasting: A forecast is made for multiple countries on the status of control. Countries reaching a control level of 100 are in control wheres countries reaching a control level of 0 are not in control.

Impact

  • Dashboard: This page contains a dashboard where instructions are given on how to use the dashboard. Information that can be deducted are the number of deaths, confirmed and tested cases, but also temperature, mobility in parks, pharmacy, groceries and retail.
  • Policy Impact: On this page an example of California and Illinois are given to explain how the graphs can be read.
