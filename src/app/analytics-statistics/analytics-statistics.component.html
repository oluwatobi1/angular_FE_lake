Infection and Death Statistics

=== COVID-19 Statistics Analysis ===
- COVID-19 infection / Deaths Percentile per category relation (Gender, Age, and relation when having other diseases) -

Below detailed Deaths Percentile per category results are based on covid19-patient-precondition-dataset which was released by the Mexican government. It include 566602 entries which is a huge number of anonymised patient and high accurate result

    COVID-19 Highest risk are:

  • 1. Patient age 75 or more, increasing death percentile to 30%. (Mexico death percentile is 6%).
  • 2. With Chronic kidney disease (renal chronic), increasing death percentile to 25%.
  • 3. With Chronic obstructive pulmonary disease (copd), inflammatory lung disease, increasing death percentile to 22%.
  • 4. With Diabetes disease, increasing death percentile to 19%.
  • 5. With Cardiovascular disease, increasing death percentile to 17%.

    Below Detailed Graphs Summaries:

  • 1. COVID-19 Infect both Genders equally, but double deaths Between Males than Females. (per infection count)
  • 2. Mexico Highest Infections, more than 35%, are between 15-30 age group. And highest deaths are between more 75+ Age group and 0-15 age group (together include more than 75% of deaths though less than 15% of infections)! Which show high deaths between kids in Mexico.
  • 3. Tobacco increase COVID-19 Death risk by 0.62%.
  • 4. Having other disease highly increase COVID-19 death risks. Increase by 20-24% in case of Renal Chronic or COPD.
